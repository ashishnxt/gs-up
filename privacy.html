QUALITY & CERTIFICATION

QUALITY –

GS ENTERPRISES is a leading manufacturer of Auto parts and sub systems for off highway equipment.
We are committed to enhance customer satisfaction and achieve sustainable growth through:

1. Manufacture and supply of consistent quality products at an optimum cost and on time delivery through mutually beneficial supplier relationship.

2.Assessment of organizational context and employee engagement.

3. Satisfy applicable requirement and Continual Improvement in all spheres of activities in our business by means first time right approach, robust manufacturing processes, up gradation of technology, people skills and practicing quality management systems.

4. Complying with safety norms and promoting an environment friendly organization via good corporate governance.

This policy shall be made available to all the employees, public and other interested parties



Quality control is not merely an obligation at GS ENTERPRISES. It constitutes the very foundation of our enduring reputation. Our single-minded commitment to quality and focus on precision dictates the best practices in manufacturing and testing processes, systems and protocol. To monitor standards and uphold the maintainence of adherence to customer specifications at all times, our facilities feature the most advanced equipment to ensure that no component that leaves our production lines falls short of expectations. Having instilled a strong belief in "Doing the Right Things Right the First Time" throughout the fabric of the company, we take great pride in not just satisfying, but in delighting our customers.
